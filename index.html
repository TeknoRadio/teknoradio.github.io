    Quality
    32Kbps = low quality and low bandwith usage
    64Kbps = ok quality for music
    128Kbps = high quality (audio CD quality) and higher bandwith usage

    Bandwith usage
    32Kbps = 14.4MB/hour
    64Kbps = 28.8MB/hour
    128Kbps = 57.6MB/hour
